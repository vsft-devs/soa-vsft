Cookies

A cookie is a small data file written to your hard drive containing information about your visit to a web page. If you prefer not to receive cookies, you may configure your browser not to accept them at all or notify and require approval before accepting new cookies. However, some web pages/sites may not function properly if the cookies are turned off, or you may have to provide the same information each time you visit those pages. Therefore, Wealthworks Partners team is not using cookies at this time.
