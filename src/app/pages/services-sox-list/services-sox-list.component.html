Using the COSO Framework for our Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) engagements, we tailor our workflows to address its core elements. In addition to ERM, organizations are increasingly focusing on two other key risk management areas: Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) and Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC).

Third Party Risk Management

TPRM involves establishing a vendor risk rating system aligned with sourcing strategies, enabling efficient vendor onboarding, classification, offboarding, and the implementation of automated solutions and vendor data lakes.

Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)

GRC focuses on an organization's approach to assessing, controlling, defining, enforcing, and monitoring risks. It integrates and coordinates these activities to ensure a cohesive and effective risk management strategy.
