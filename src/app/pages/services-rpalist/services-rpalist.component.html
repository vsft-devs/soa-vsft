SOX Testing

Automation

We can create customized solutions for your SOX areas because we have technical accounting knowledge and domain expertise in SOX.

Following automation, each journal entry is put through a weekly battery of automated data analysis tests:

  • In addition to checking controls, tests search for a variety of indicators of control weakness.
  • The test's outcomes are then assigned a risk score.
  • The internal audit team and an overall control owner are automatically notified when journal entries with high-risk scores need to be reviewed.
