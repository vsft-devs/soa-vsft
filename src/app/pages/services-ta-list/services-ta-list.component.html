Revenue

Recognition

We have supported numerous companies in implementing ASC 606, Revenue from Contracts with Customers (The “Standard”), and offer the following benefits:

  • Industry Expertise - Deep knowledge across various industries to address your unique challenges.
  • Comprehensive Capabilities - Access to experts in process optimization, ERP systems, and data analysis.
  • Pragmatic Approach - A focus on identifying opportunities to enhance revenue processes and systems, including accounting policies, personnel, financial reporting, and business operations.
  • Collaborative Working Style - Partnering closely with your team to achieve seamless and effective implementation.

We provide predictive models that can identify potential discrepancies in revenue recognition. We will evaluate your requirements and help develop a comprehensive implementation plan for the Standard. This includes:

  • Identifying key impacts on revenue streams and contracts.
  • Addressing critical accounting, billing, and other related issues.
  • Detecting data gaps and defining new or revised control and process requirements.
  • Creating and executing a plan for the successful adoption of the Standard.
  • Resolving additional matters, such as the capitalization, amortization, and impairment of internal-use software development costs.

Our goal is to ensure a seamless and thorough implementation process.
